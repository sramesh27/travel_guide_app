POPULAR PLACES

(most popular and beautful places that cover your eyes)

Aquarium

India’s first and largest walk-through aquarium housing everything from small to big aquatic animals. This Marine Kingdom is the fourth in the many kingdoms unveiled by VGP.

MGM Dizzee

MGM Dizzee World is one of the largest and oldest amusement parks in India. It has an aqua park, fitted with various fun-filled rides and activities like artificial ocean waves etc..

Guindy National Park

It is the 8th-smallest National Park of India and one of the very few national parks situated inside a city. Main attraction of the park is the 20 million years old Specimen of Tyrannosaurus.
